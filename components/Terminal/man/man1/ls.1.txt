ls(1)

[1mNAME[0m
    ls - list directory contents

[1mSYNOPSIS[0m
    ls [[3mOPTION[0m]... [[3mFILE[0m]...

[1mDESCRIPTION[0m
    List information about the FILEs (the current directory by default). Sorts entries alphabetically.

    [1m-a[0m, [1m--all[0m
        do not ignore entries starting with .

    [1m-A[0m, [1m--almost-all[0m
        do not list implied . and ..
